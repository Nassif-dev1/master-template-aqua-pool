About AquaPro Pool Services

Family-Owned. Houston-Proud. Pool-Obsessed.

A Houston Journey Built on Crystal Clear Standards

AquaPro began with a single truck and a simple mission: to provide Houston families with the highest standard of pool care without the stress. Over the last two decades, we have grown into the region's premier maintenance provider by treating every pool as if it were in our own backyard.

Our growth is rooted in local expertise. We understand the specific challenges Houston's climate poses to pool chemistry and equipment. From the humidity of July to the unexpected freezes of winter, our team is trained to keep your oasis perfect year-round.
